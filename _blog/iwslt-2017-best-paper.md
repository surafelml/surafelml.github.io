Our paper **[Improving Zero-Shot Translation of Low-Resource Languages](https://aclanthology.org/2017.iwslt-1.16/)** received the **Best Paper Award** at the 14th International Workshop on Spoken Language Translation (IWSLT 2017), Tokyo.

> IWSLT 2017 Best Paper Award was given to Surafel Melaku Lakew, Marcello Federico, Matteo Negri, Marco Turchi and Quintino Francesco Lotito — *Improving Zero-Shot Translation of Low-Resource Languages*.
