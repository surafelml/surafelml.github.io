### Bio 
About Surafel Melaku LAKEW

<p> Surafel M. Lakew is an applied scientist at 
<a href="https://www.amazon.science"> Amazon AWS AI Labs</a>, since 2020. 
He completed his Ph.D. at the Fondazione Bruno Kessler 
<a href="https://ict.fbk.eu/units/hlt-mt/">HLT-MT</a> research lab 
and at the University of Trento <a href="http://ict.unitn.it/">ICT-Doctoral School</a>, 
advised by <a href="https://www.marcellofederico.net/"> Marcello Federico</a>. 
His thesis, <a href="https://github.com/surafelml/phd-thesis"> Multilingual Neural Machine Translation for Low-Resource Languages</a>, 
investigates approaches to improve the performance of neural machine translation models for language pairs without large-scale training data.
</p>

<p>
After obtaining his bachelor’s degree from <a href="https://ju.edu.et/"> Jimma University</a>, 
he worked as an assistant lecturer at <a href="https://bdu.edu.et/"> Bahir Dar University</a>, 
Ethiopia. Before starting his Ph.D., he received his M.Sc. in computer science 
from the <a href="http://www.unive.it/pag/13526/">University of Venice</a> under the 
supervision of Prof. <a href="http://www.dsi.unive.it/~pelillo/">Marcello Pelillo</a> and 
Prof. <a href="http://www.ce.unipr.it/people/aprati/">Andrea Prati</a>, and 
an MBA from <a href="https://www.bbs.unibo.eu/">Bologna Business School</a> 
in Green Management and Sustainable Businesses. 
</p>
