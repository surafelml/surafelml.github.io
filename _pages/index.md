Hi, welcome to my page! I'm Surafel, currently an [applied scientist at Amazon AI](https://www.amazon.science/author/surafel-melaku-lakew).


Previously, I was a doctoral candidate at the <a href="http://ict.unitn.it/">ICT Doctoral School</a> University of Trento and 
at the <a href="https://ict.fbk.eu/units/hlt-mt">HLT-MT lab</a> Fondazione Bruno Kessler. 
My dissertation focuses on [Multilingual Neural Machine Translation](https://github.com/surafelml/phd-thesis), 
particularly for low-resource languages.
